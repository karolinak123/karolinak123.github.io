What does influence fuel consumption based on mtcars dataset - DDP Project

6th of December 2018

Application Capability

The capability of this application is to show what factors and how influence fuel consumption based on 'mtcars' dataset.

Data - mtcars dataset

The data was extracted from the 1974 Motor Trend US magazine, and comprises fuel consumption and 10 aspects of automobile design and performance for 32 automobiles (1973-74 models).

Usage

User can choose one of the variables from: -Cylinders - Number of cylinders
-Transmission - 0 = automatic, 1 = manual
-Horsepower - Gross horsepower
-Weight - 1000 lbs unit
-Number of forward gears
-¼ mile time,
and compare it with Miles/(US) gallon for 32 automobiles.

In addition, chart is broken down by transmission type (0 is automatic, and 1 is manual.)

Example of scatter chart

require(ggplot2)
p<-ggplot(mtcars, aes_string(x=mtcars$wt, y=mtcars$mpg, color = as.factor(mtcars$am)))
p<-p + geom_point() + 
  labs(x="Weight" , y="Miles Per Gallon")
p$labels$colour <-"Transmission"
print(p)
